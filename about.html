Effluent Treatment Plant (ETP)

At Agile Ventures, we specialize in manufacturing and servicing Effluent Treatment Plants (ETPs) tailored to diverse industrial needs. Our ETP solutions efficiently remove pollutants from industrial effluents, ensuring compliance with environmental regulations and promoting sustainability. We provide comprehensive installation, maintenance, and support services for seamless operations.

Sewage Treatment Plant (STP)

At Agile Ventures, we excel in manufacturing and servicing Sewage Treatment Plants (STPs) designed to treat domestic and municipal sewage effectively. Our STP solutions utilize advanced technologies to remove contaminants and ensure the safe discharge or reuse of treated water, promoting environmental sustainability. We offer comprehensive installation, maintenance, and support services.

Industrial RO plant

Agile Ventures specializes in manufacturing and servicing Industrial Reverse Osmosis (RO) plants, delivering high-quality purified water for various industrial applications. Our RO plants remove impurities, minerals, and contaminants, ensuring water meets stringent quality standards. We provide tailored solutions, installation, maintenance, and ongoing support to optimize plant performance.

Industrial Softener Plant

At Agile Ventures, we design, manufacture, and service Industrial Water Softener Plants tailored to address hard water challenges in industrial settings. Our softener plants use advanced ion exchange technology to remove calcium, magnesium, and other minerals, preventing scaling and ensuring efficient industrial processes. We offer customized solutions and comprehensive support services.

Demineralization (DM) Plant

At Agile Ventures, we specialize in manufacturing and servicing Demineralization (DM) Plants, providing high-quality water purification solutions for industrial applications. Our DM plants use ion exchange resins to remove dissolved minerals, producing demineralized water for various processes. We offer customized designs, installation, maintenance, and ongoing support to ensure optimal performance.

Organic Waste Composters (OWC)

At Agile Ventures, we manufacture and service Organic Waste Composters (OWC) designed to convert organic waste into nutrient-rich compost. Our OWC systems utilize advanced composting technology to decompose organic matter efficiently, minimizing waste and producing compost for soil enrichment. We provide customized solutions, installation, maintenance, and ongoing support for sustainable waste management.
